# Reality Transurfing Summary
**Author:** Vadim Zeland

Reality Transurfing is a mental model that suggests we live in a "multiverse" of possibilities. Success is not about fighting for what you want, but "choosing" the desired reality from the **Space of Variations**.

---

## 1. The Space of Variations
*   **Definition:** An infinite field containing all possible scripts and paths (past, present, and future).
*   **The Choice:** You do not "create" reality through hard work; you move your "point of realization" to a different sector by changing your energy frequency.

## 2. Pendulums (Energy Structures)
*   **Nature:** Created when groups of people think in the same direction (religions, corporations, sports fans).
*   **The Trap:** Pendulums are "energy vampires" that thrive on your emotion—whether positive or negative.
*   **Strategy:** To stop losing energy, you must become "empty" (indifferent) or "extinguish" them by reacting in an unexpected, humorous way.

## 3. Excessive Potential & Importance
*   **The Problem:** When you give something too much "Importance," you create **Excessive Potential**.
*   **Equilibrium Forces:** Nature hates imbalances. If you want something too much (or fear it too much), Equilibrium Forces will intervene to balance the energy, usually by depriving you of the object.
*   **The Fix:** Lower the importance. Treat your goal with the same casual certainty as going to a mailbox to pick up the mail.

## 4. Internal vs. External Intent
*   **Internal Intent:** The desire to *act* on the world (willpower). It is limited and exhausting.
*   **External Intent:** The "determination to have." It is the force that shifts the world around you to match your mental "slide."

## 5. Unity of Heart and Mind
*   **The Rule:** External Intent only works when the Soul (feelings) and the Mind (thoughts) are in agreement. 
*   **The Signal:** If your mind says "yes" but your soul feels a quiet sense of "no" (the **Rustle of Morning Stars**), the sector you are choosing is wrong for you.

## 6. Target Slides
*   **Technique:** Instead of visualizing the process, visualize the **end result** as if it has already happened. 
*   **Integration:** You must place yourself *inside* the slide and live it mentally, rather than looking at it as a distant screen.

## 7. Coordination of Intention
*   **The Concept:** If you choose to view a seemingly negative event as positive, you shift onto a positive life line.
*   **Mantra:** "My world takes care of me."

---

### Core Maxims
1. **Don't fight the world:** Just change your "frequency."
2. **Let go of Importance:** If you don't care about the result, you get the result.
3. **Be the Observer:** Watch the pendulums without getting hooked.
